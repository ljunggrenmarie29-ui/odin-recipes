Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's the ultimate comfort food, perfect for feeding a crowd.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F)
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel and cheese in a baking dish
  5. Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbling
  7. Let rest 15 minutes before serving
