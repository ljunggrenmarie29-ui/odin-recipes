Margherita Pizza

Description

A classic Neapolitan pizza with a thin crispy base, fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella and basil. Simple, perfect, iconic.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F)
  2. Stretch dough into a round base
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly
  4. Tear mozzarella and place on top
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden
  6. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil
