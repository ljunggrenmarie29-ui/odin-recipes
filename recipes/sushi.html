Sushi Rolls

Description

Fresh homemade sushi rolls filled with cucumber, avocado and your choice of fish. Fun to make and even better to eat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 200g fresh salmon or tuna
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cucumber
  • Soy sauce and wasabi to serve

Steps

  1. Cook and season sushi rice, let cool
  2. Place nori sheet on a bamboo mat
  3. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori
  4. Add fish, avocado and cucumber in a line
  5. Roll tightly using the mat
  6. Slice into pieces with a sharp wet knife
  7. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi
